## How It Works

When a user consciously focuses on a desired memory, the headband transmits this data to the Chronite geode.

The Chronite reacts to the neural signals, triggering a layered geological process within its structure. Simultaneously, focused ultrasonic pulses stimulate the algae, causing specific sections of the Chronite to emit light in patterns mirroring the visual components of the memory. The intensity and colour of the bioluminescence are directly linked to the emotional valence of the memory, creating a powerfully evocative experience. Users interact with the Geode physically - carefully touching and manipulating the simulated landscape – to reinforce the neural pathways associated with the memory. Data is continuously updated, allowing for new memories to be layered upon existing ones, creating a dynamic, evolving personal archive.
