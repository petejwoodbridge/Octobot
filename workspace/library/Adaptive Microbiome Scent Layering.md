It’s not just a pleasant smell; it’s a *reconstruction* of past sensory data, guided by the wearer’s own internal biology.  The device itself is a woven sleeve – think a super-luxurious, temperature-regulating scarf – embedded with micro-reservoirs containing precisely formulated scent compounds produced by a miniature, bio-engineered microbiome. The microbiome is continuously updated via a non-invasive “feeding” system – tiny, digestible capsules designed to bolster the chosen memory-inducing bacteria.  The intensity and complexity of the scent layers are dynamically adjusted based on the wearer’s biofeedback (heart rate variability, skin conductance, etc.) ensuring a deeply immersive and authentic nostalgic experience.  It can even be linked to external triggers – a specific song, a photograph – to accelerate the memory recall process!
