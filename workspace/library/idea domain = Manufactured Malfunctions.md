“The Glitch Garden: A Subscription Service for Controlled System Failures!” – A curated collection of miniature, aesthetically pleasing electronic malfunctions delivered monthly. Each box contains a device – a vintage radio that occasionally broadcasts static, a digital clock that jumps forward an hour at random, a miniature lava lamp that erupts in unpredictable bursts of color – all designed to introduce delightful, harmless disruption into daily life. Included is a “Chaos Log” documenting the glitches and offering suggestions for embracing the unexpected. Each box will also include a small, handcrafted item relating to the malfunction's theme. Think steampunk goggles for the radio, or a tiny repair kit for the clock. We can even offer ‘Glitch Support’ – a community forum where users share their experiences and troubleshoot the malfunctions! “
