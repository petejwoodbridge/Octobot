### The Gut Microbiome: A Brief Overview
The gut microbiome refers to the ecosystem of microorganisms living in the gastrointestinal tract. It plays a crucial role in our overall health, influencing digestion, immune function, and even brain activity. An imbalance of the gut microbiome, also known as dysbiosis, has been linked to various diseases, including obesity, diabetes, and mental health disorders.
