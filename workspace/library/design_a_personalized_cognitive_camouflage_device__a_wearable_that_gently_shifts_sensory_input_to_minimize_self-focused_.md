## Overview

## The Problem It Solves
Chronic anxiety and rumination – the persistent, involuntary dwelling on negative thoughts – significantly impact mental wellbeing. Current therapies often require conscious effort and behavioral changes, which can be difficult to maintain. Chronosync offers a passive, non-intrusive solution, intervening *before* intense rumination fully takes hold, allowing the wearer to re-establish a sense of grounding and perspective. It addresses the lack of immediate intervention technology available for managing anxiety symptoms.

These pulses subtly shift the wearer’s perception of time, creating a feeling of spaciousness and reducing the sharpness of internal thoughts.

The geode’s outer shell is constructed from a bio-reactive polymer that changes its temperature based on the wearer's physiological data, captured through integrated biosensors. This feedback loop allows the micro-robotics to refine their stimulation in real-time – essentially creating a personalized ‘soundscape of calm’ adapted to the individual’s specific state.
