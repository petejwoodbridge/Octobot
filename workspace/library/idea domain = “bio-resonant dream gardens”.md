## How It Works
The Bio Resonant Dream Garden centers around a geodesic dome constructed from a reinforced, translucent bio-polymer infused with genetically modified, bioluminescent fungi – *Mycillum Lux*. These fungi are carefully cultivated to emit a spectrum of colors based on pre-programmed resonant frequencies. The core of the system is the “Somni-Core,” a palm-sized device resembling a polished river stone. This Somni-Core contains a miniature quantum entanglement sensor that constantly analyzes the wearer’s bio-signals – heart rate variability, brainwave patterns, and subtle muscle tension – through a non-invasive skin patch. 

This data is fed into a sophisticated AI, “Nightingale,” which then dynamically adjusts the Mycillum Lux’s color output and, crucially, generates a complex, layered soundscape. Nightingale doesn’t simply play pre-recorded music; it creates an evolving sonic tapestry using micro-speakers embedded within the dome’s structure. These speakers utilize *Sonaris Echoes* – microscopic, bio-engineered algae that vibrate in response to the sound waves, further modulating the light patterns. 

To deepen the dream-inducing effect, the dome is equipped with a network of pressure sensors beneath the floor, which detect the wearer’s subtle movements and translate them into rhythmic shifts in the bioluminescence and sound.  Finally, a subtle, oscillating magnetic field – generated by a discreet base unit – is applied to induce gentle theta brainwaves, priming the mind for relaxation and potentially, lucid dreaming. The entire system is controlled via a neural interface headband, allowing the user to subtly influence the garden’s responses and even guide their dream content.
