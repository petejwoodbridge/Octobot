## The Problem It Solves

Traditional composting is a largely passive process. We throw food scraps into a bin, wait, and hope for the best. But what if the waste itself had a voice? What if we could optimize the process based on the compost’s actual *desire* for certain conditions? Furthermore, a significant portion of organic waste ends up in landfills, contributing to methane emissions and wasted potential. We're essentially treating organic material as a problem to be solved, rather than a complex, living system.
