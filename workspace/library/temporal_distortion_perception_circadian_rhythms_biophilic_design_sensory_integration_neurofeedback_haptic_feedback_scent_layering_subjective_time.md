## The Problem It Solves: The Temporal Drift

Modern life is a relentless assault on our natural circadian rhythms. Blue light, irregular schedules, and synthetic environments have fractured our internal clocks, leading to “temporal drift” – the subjective feeling that time is speeding up, slowing down, or otherwise distorting. Existing solutions like light therapy and sleep trackers treat the *symptoms* but not the root cause: a misaligned internal temporal map.
