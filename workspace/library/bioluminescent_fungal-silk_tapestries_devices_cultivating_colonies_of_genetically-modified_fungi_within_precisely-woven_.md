## The Problem It Solves
Current air quality monitoring devices are often bulky, inaccurate, and fail to translate data into an easily digestible, emotionally resonant experience. Individuals, particularly those with respiratory sensitivities or living in heavily polluted areas, lack a constant, accessible, and genuinely informative way to gauge the quality of the air they breathe. Existing “smart” air purifiers offer a reactive solution, but don't provide predictive or contextual awareness of the environment.

The frequency and amplitude of these vibrations are calibrated to represent different toxicity levels.
