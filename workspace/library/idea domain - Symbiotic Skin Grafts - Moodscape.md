Critically, it also utilizes a micro-patch of genetically modified *Lactobacillus* bacteria, specifically engineered to respond to these HRV fluctuations. 

When the Chrysalis Node detects elevated stress levels, it triggers a cascade. Firstly, it stimulates the bacteria to release a precisely controlled amount of GABA (gamma-aminobutyric acid), a naturally occurring neurotransmitter known for its calming effects. These needles don't inject anything; instead, they deliver a gentle, oscillating stream of low-frequency sonic vibrations – specifically tuned to resonate with the human vagus nerve, the primary nerve responsible for regulating the parasympathetic nervous system (the “rest and digest” response). The Serenity Weave itself is constructed from a blend of Merino wool and a proprietary bio-polymer that enhances conductivity and allows for seamless communication between the Node, bacteria, and the user’s skin.
