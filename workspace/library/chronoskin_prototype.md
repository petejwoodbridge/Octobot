ChronoSkin addresses this gap by offering a tangible, immersive pathway to historical and personal experiences.

The resonators output these soundscapes, which are then translated into specific vibrations felt through the suit's bio-reactive layers. These layers, constructed from a proprietary polymer matrix, respond to these vibrations, shifting color (using bioluminescent dyes), subtly altering temperature, and creating nuanced textural changes – mimicking the environmental conditions of the chosen temporal signature.
