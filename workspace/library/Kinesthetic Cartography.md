# GeoSense: The Weathering Floor – A kinetic floor system that translates real-time meteorological data into dynamically shifting haptic sensations. The system utilizes a network of piezoelectric sensors embedded within a bio-luminescent algae-infused, recycled plastic substrate. Algae bioluminescence reacts to temperature changes - warm weather creates gentle undulating warmth, while cold temperatures produce a subtle pulsing chill. Rainfall triggers a shifting pattern of pressure, simulating the sensation of rain. Wind speed influences the amplitude and rhythm of the floor's movement. Users wear a haptic suit that amplifies and translates these sensations into a personalized experience. The floor's patterns are displayed on a companion app, allowing users to “navigate” weather systems visually and kinetically. It’s a ‘rainy’ day on your feet!
