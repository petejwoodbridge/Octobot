“Project: SylvaVest – Bio-Integrated Tax Credits” – “SylvaVest is a network of bio-sensors and micro-robotics deployed within forests and natural habitats. These robots, powered by piezoelectric energy harvested from the environment (wind, rain, vibrations), monitor and *actively manage* plant health, biodiversity, and overall ecosystem resilience.  Crucially, successful ecological restoration—increased tree cover, restored waterways, thriving insect populations—generates 'Eco-Credits' that users can ‘spend’ on things! Think offsetting your carbon footprint with a beautifully restored grove of ancient oaks, or funding the reintroduction of a native pollinator species.  The system is entirely decentralized, governed by a blockchain-based 'Root Network' – guaranteeing transparency and preventing manipulation.  It’s like… paying your taxes *to* the planet!”
