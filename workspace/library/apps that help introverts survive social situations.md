“The Shell Navigator: A Personalized Social Simulation App” - A mobile app that creates realistic, low-pressure social simulations for introverts to practice conversations and social scenarios. Users build avatars, choose scenarios (coffee shop, networking event, etc.), and interact with AI ‘social bots’ that adapt to their responses. The app tracks anxiety levels via biofeedback data and provides personalized coping strategies and post-simulation reflection exercises. It’s like a social playground, built for cautious explorers!
