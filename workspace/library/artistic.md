## ChromaSculpt

## Overview
ChromaSculpt is a wearable device – a flexible, bio-reactive mesh garment – that allows the wearer to dynamically sculpt their appearance using projected light and subtle shifts in their own skin pigmentation. It’s like living, breathing, customizable makeup, responding directly to the wearer’s emotions and movements.

## The Problem It Solves
Traditional makeup is static, messy, and can feel restrictive. It requires constant upkeep and can often exacerbate insecurities by focusing on perceived flaws. Furthermore, makeup trends are fleeting, leading to unnecessary waste and the pressure to constantly conform. ChromaSculpt provides a truly personalized, adaptable aesthetic experience, eliminating the need for traditional products and empowering individuals to express themselves in a fluid and intuitive way.

## How It Works
The ChromaSculpt garment is constructed from a conductive, bio-compatible mesh woven with microscopic, addressable LED diodes and micro-pigment capsules. These components are powered by a small, rechargeable energy cell integrated into the garment’s core. The system utilizes a sophisticated AI algorithm – “Muse” – which learns the wearer’s aesthetic preferences, physiological responses (heart rate, skin temperature), and movement patterns. 

Muse then projects intricate patterns of light onto the garment’s surface through the LED diodes, creating dynamic colour shifts and illusions. Simultaneously, it controls the release of micro-pigment capsules in specific areas of the mesh, subtly altering the wearer's skin tone. The garment reacts in real-time to the wearer's emotional state – for example, shifting to calming blues when stressed or vibrant reds during moments of excitement – or to external stimuli, like music or ambient lighting. The level of pigment change is controlled by haptic feedback sensors woven into the garment that detect movement and pressure, ensuring a seamless blend with the wearer’s natural complexion. The AI learns and adapts the patterns and pigment levels over time, creating truly bespoke and responsive aesthetics.
