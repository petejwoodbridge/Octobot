## Overview

It cultivates miniature, self-modifying fungal colonies within a dynamically controlled chamber, translating their complex growth cycles – including pigment shifts, texture changes, and spore release – into a constantly evolving, multi-sensory landscape of scent and touch.

It addresses the human tendency to struggle with concepts outside our immediate experience.

2. **Pigment Modulation:** Controlled LED lighting activates specific genetic pathways within the fungi, causing shifts in color and texture – simulating the deposition of minerals, the formation of strata, or volcanic activity.
3. **Spore Dispersion Control:** The AI releases carefully timed bursts of fungal spores, creating miniature “storm clouds” that interact with the chamber’s surface, further altering texture and scattering pigments.

The entire experience is viewed through a multi-layered sensory interface: a tactile surface mimicking the “ground” beneath the fungal bloom, and a directional scent diffuser providing a constantly shifting olfactory panorama.
