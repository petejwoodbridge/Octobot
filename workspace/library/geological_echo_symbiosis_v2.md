These geodes are networked via a subterranean “Neural-Stone” grid – a mesh of interwoven, bio-conductive quartz – that collects and amplifies these resonant signals. Furthermore, embedded biosensors within the geodes continuously monitor the surrounding rock’s structural integrity, feeding this data back into the system for real-time geological health assessments.
