## Overview
MoodBrew is a smart coffee maker that utilizes biosensors and AI to analyze your real-time mood and physiological state – heart rate variability, skin conductivity, and even subtle facial expression analysis – to craft the *perfect* cup of coffee, dynamically adjusting brewing parameters to optimize for your needs. Forget pre-programmed settings; MoodBrew learns your coffee preferences intimately, becoming your personal caffeine curator.

## The Problem It Solves
The current coffee market is based on static profiles. People choose roast levels, brew strengths, and bean origins based on generalized preferences ("I like strong coffee" or "I like light, fruity coffee"). This rarely aligns with an individual’s fluctuating mood or energy needs. Often, people end up with a cup of coffee that either doesn't address their immediate needs (too weak when tired, too intense when stressed) or, conversely, they’re forcing a specific brew style that they don't actually enjoy at that moment. There’s no system that intuitively caters to the nuance of human experience.

## How It Works
MoodBrew consists of three key components: a smart coffee maker unit, a wrist-worn biosensor (similar in size to a fitness tracker), and a cloud-based AI engine. 

1.  **Biosensor Data:** The wrist sensor continuously monitors heart rate variability (HRV), skin conductance (measuring sweat gland activity – a proxy for stress), and utilizes a miniature, low-resolution camera to analyze subtle facial muscle movements (micro-expressions). This data is transmitted wirelessly to the coffee maker.

2.  **AI Analysis:** The coffee maker’s integrated AI engine (a custom-trained neural network) receives the biosensor data. Based on pre-programmed algorithms and machine learning, the AI interprets the user's state – categorizing it as "Focused," "Relaxed," "Stressed," “Low Energy,” or “Creative.”

3.  **Dynamic Brewing:**  Based on the identified state, MoodBrew adjusts its brewing parameters *in real-time*. It can:
    *   Increase water temperature (up to 96°C) for a stronger, more stimulating brew during "Low Energy" or "Stressed" states.
    *   Decrease water temperature for a smoother, more nuanced experience during “Relaxed” states.
    *   Modify bloom time and water flow rate to extract specific flavor profiles based on perceived “Focused” or "Creative" moods.
    *   Even subtly infuse small amounts of adaptogenic herbs (ashwagandha, rhodiola) via micro-dosing during periods of stress.

The system also includes a 'Learning Mode' where the AI learns the user’s preferred taste profiles *within* each identified state – e.g., a “Focused” brew might prefer a slightly brighter roast than a typical “Focused” brew.
