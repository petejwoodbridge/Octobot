## How It Works
Chronoscent operates on a three-stage process, meticulously designed for maximum dream-to-scent fidelity. First, the user dons the ‘Somnus Band,’ a lightweight, wrist-worn device equipped with a high-density EEG sensor and a subtle vibrational pulse emitter. As you fall asleep, the Somnus Band continuously monitors your brainwave activity, specifically focusing on delta waves – the dominant frequency associated with deep, vivid dreaming. 

Second, the data from the Somnus Band is transmitted wirelessly to the Chronoscent Chamber. Inside, the ‘Neuro-Aroma’ core is the heart of the device. This core houses a microfluidic array seeded with millions of genetically-modified ‘Aroma-Neurons’ – tiny, bio-engineered cells derived from *Dianthus* and *Jasmine* plants. These Aroma-Neurons have been specifically trained through a process of biofeedback reinforcement to respond to specific patterns of neural activity. As the chamber detects your dream’s dominant emotional state (joy, fear, sadness, etc.), it triggers the corresponding Aroma-Neurons.

Finally, a laser-guided micro-dispersion system precisely controls the release of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) generated by the activated Aroma-Neurons. These VOCs are then captured and refined through a miniature chromatography column, isolating the key scent molecules associated with your dream. The chamber then gently diffuses these purified scents into the room, creating an olfactory experience perfectly attuned to the narrative unfolding in your subconscious. The entire process takes approximately 60 seconds to calibrate and then continuously adjusts based on your ongoing brainwave activity.
