Third, the micro-controller analyzes data from the filament sensors and the moss’s fluorescence. It then controls the flow of the bio-polymer solution, triggering the moss to bloom and fade in specific patterns, visually representing the wearer’s physiological state. The system operates on a closed-loop feedback system – the moss's response influences the SMA’s movement, and vice versa, creating a dynamic, living textile.
