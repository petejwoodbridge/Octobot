Think of it like tuning a radio to a specific frequency, but instead of sound, we’re capturing the echoes of plant life. 

Next, this data is fed into the Chronarium. These Seedling Vectors utilize a proprietary bio-ceramic coating, allowing them to penetrate even the most compacted soil layers. 

Finally, the Chronarium activates a low-frequency vibrational field, amplified by the Seedling Vectors, to ‘wake up’ the dormant epigenetic markers within the soil. This gentle stimulation encourages root growth, microbial activity, and ultimately, the resumption of the historical plant community’s metabolic processes. We’re essentially giving the soil a gentle nudge, telling it, "Remember who you once were, and thrive again!"
