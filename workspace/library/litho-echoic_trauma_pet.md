Existing biofeedback devices offer data but lack a truly *experiential* bridge to understanding the deeply rooted patterns of a traumatic event. The LETP directly addresses this by translating the complex neural signatures of trauma into a controlled, kinesthetic experience, offering a potentially more accessible and profoundly impactful approach to processing painful memories. It fills the unexplored territory of translating emotional data into a tangible, interactive pathway for cognitive restructuring.
