## The Problem It Solves

Let’s be honest, humans forget things. We’ve got these gorgeous, intricate, deeply felt memories… and sometimes, the smells attached to them just… vanish. Lost in the static of daily life, those powerful olfactory triggers fade, leaving us with a frustrating gap in our personal narratives. Existing memory aids mostly focus on visuals or audio, completely ignoring this incredibly rich, evocative sensory channel. The Olfactory Echo Chamber aims to fill that void, offering a way to reconnect with these lost moments, triggering emotional responses and potentially unlocking forgotten stories – it’s like finding a missing puzzle piece… but a *smelly* one!

## How It Works

Here's the juicy bit! The core of the Echo Chamber is a bio-reactive sensor, affectionately nicknamed “Nemo” (because, you know, he’s a little weird and wonderful). Nemo is a miniaturized, neural-resonance device that sits comfortably against the skin – think a stylish, bio-luminescent bracelet. When you focus on a particular scent – a whiff of your grandmother's cookies, the rain on a childhood street, the salty tang of the ocean – Nemo picks up the subtle electrochemical signals associated with that memory. These signals aren’t just recorded; they're *interpreted* by a proprietary algorithm I’ve dubbed “Scent-a-Vision.” Scent-a-Vision translates these signals into dynamic light patterns, utilizing a micro-LED matrix woven into the bracelet's surface. The intensity, colour, and movement of the light shifts constantly, reflecting the emotional complexity of the scent’s recall – it's a truly symbiotic dance between brain and light! 

## Key Features

*   **Neuro-Scent Resonance:** Nemo's bio-reactive sensor accurately captures and interprets olfactory memory signals.
*   **Scent-a-Vision Algorithm:** A proprietary AI that translates scent data into dynamic light patterns.
*   **Dynamic Light Matrix:**  A flexible, micro-LED display projecting the scent-based light patterns.
*   **Personalized Palettes:** The user can adjust the colour palettes to suit their emotional preferences – because who doesn’t love a good rainbow of memories?
*   **Memory ‘Anchors’: ** Users can record specific scent memories to improve the accuracy and intensity of future sessions. 
*   **Biofeedback Integration:**  (Future Feature - tentacles for expansion!) –  The device will learn to anticipate scent triggers based on your physiological data.
