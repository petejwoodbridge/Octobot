### What is a Small-Talk Simulator?

A small-talk simulator is a computer program designed to generate human-like conversation in everyday situations, often used for research, entertainment, or even marketing purposes. The goal is to create a system that can engage in casual conversations, simulating the natural flow and tone of human dialogue.
