Based on this analysis, the algorithms precisely control the intensity and color patterns of the *Photoplankton Lux* algae. Furthermore, ChronoWeave incorporates micro-actuators that subtly manipulate the fabric’s texture, creating a corresponding haptic feedback experience – a gentle ripple simulating a tide or a soft shift mirroring the movement of leaves. Finally, a secondary layer of electrochromic pigments allows the fabric to mimic octopus camouflage, shifting shades to blend with its surroundings, driven by the same sensory data.
