## How It Works

The core of the Litho Sonantic Echo Garden resides within its ‘Litho-Matrix,’ a meticulously sculpted arrangement of layered, phosphorescent shale and basalt. This isn’t just aesthetic; the shale contains embedded piezo-electric sensors, incredibly sensitive to subtle vibrations – everything from a heartbeat to the tension in a clenched fist. These vibrations feed into the ‘Harmonic Engine,’ a bespoke device built around a modified quantum processor. 

The Harmonic Engine then translates these vibrations into a complex sonic landscape – not just music, but precisely calibrated soundwaves based on fractal geometry and bioacoustic principles. This sonification isn’t random; it's designed to interact with the plants within the garden. We've cultivated a unique species of genetically-modified *Lumenflora* – a moss-like organism – that reacts directly to the soundwaves. The Lumenflora’s bioluminescence pulses and shifts in color based on the frequency and amplitude of the sonification, creating a visual echo of the user’s emotions. 

Furthermore, integrated within the Litho-Matrix are micro-irrigation channels infused with mood-altering essential oils – lavender for calming, citrus for uplifting, sandalwood for grounding – dispensed based on the sonic output.  A small, solar-powered ‘Bio-Synchronizer’ monitors the overall health of the Lumenflora and adjusts the sonification in real-time, creating a dynamic feedback loop.  It’s a closed-loop system where your emotions shape the garden, and the garden, in turn, helps you understand and process those emotions.
