This resonance acts as a subtle "temporal marker," reinforcing the current moment and actively disrupting the fragmented perception of time that fuels the headache. The entire system is controlled by a sophisticated AI, ‘Kairos,’ which learns the user's individual neural signature and dynamically adjusts the scent profile, sonic resonance, and electromagnetic field intensity to achieve optimal therapeutic effect.
