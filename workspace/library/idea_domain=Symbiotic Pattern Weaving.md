**How It Works**

Okay, brace yourselves! We’re using a specially cultivated strain of *Mycena luxaeterna* (the "eternal light mushroom") interwoven with a network of ultra-fine, bio-compatible silk threads. These silk threads act as conduits, delivering a nutrient-rich solution – a bespoke cocktail of sugars, vitamins, and trace minerals – directly to the fungal colonies.  The silk is then meticulously woven into complex geometric patterns, utilizing robotic looms programmed with fractal algorithms.  Crucially, each weave segment is ‘seeded’ with a carefully selected colony of fungi.  As the fungi thrive, they produce bioluminescent light, creating patterns that shift and change based on environmental factors – temperature, humidity, light levels – and, incredibly, subtle shifts in human emotions detected via a miniature, non-invasive biofeedback sensor woven into the garment. Think of it as a conversation with your clothes!
