Here's the juicy bit: The cake incorporates a miniature, wireless sensor array (think tiny, edible electrodes) that continuously monitors the user's heart rate variability (HRV). The entire process is controlled by a ridiculously cute, palm-sized ‘Calm Controller’ – a repurposed seashell with a touch screen!
