## How It Works
The core of ChronoBloom lies in genetically modified *Bryum chronos*, a moss species engineered to exhibit distinct vibrational patterns correlating to environmental variables and, crucially, the passage of time. These moss colonies are housed within a series of interconnected, sealed chambers – initially focused on recreating the Cambrian Period – each precisely calibrated to mimic the temperature, humidity, light levels, and atmospheric composition of that era. Simultaneously, a haptic glove allows the user to feel the moss’s texture and subtle movements, augmenting the auditory experience.
