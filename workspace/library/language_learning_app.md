“LinguaBloom: The Symbiotic Language Garden” – A VR experience where users cultivate a fantastical garden by speaking a new language. Incorrect pronunciations cause plants to wilt, while accurate ones trigger vibrant blooms and unique, fantastical flora. The app tracks user progress not by “correctness,” but by the *diversity* of plant life in their garden – a testament to their adventurous linguistic exploration! Features include: “Pollination Challenges” (short, gamified conversations), “Root Systems” (detailed pronunciation feedback visualized as glowing root networks), “Bloom Events” (unlocking new, exotic plants with complex vocabulary), and “Guardian Spirits” – quirky, AI-powered spirits who offer encouragement (and occasionally, hilariously misguided advice!).  It incorporates biofeedback – plant growth is directly influenced by the user's excitement level.
