Crucially, the garment subtly shifts the *temperature* of the surrounding air, simulating a cool, damp breeze. The entire effect is layered – visual, tactile, and thermal – to mimic the psychological and physical experience of being caught in a rainstorm.

By directly translating internal states into a captivating, controllable environmental simulation, the Chromatic Nimbus offers a proactive, artistic, and deeply personal way to manage anxiety and find comfort in the beauty of a simulated storm.
