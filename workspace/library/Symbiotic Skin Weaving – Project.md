**How It Works**

It's wonderfully weird! We’ll cultivate specialized strains of *Photobacterium phosphoreum* (a bioluminescent bacteria, naturally!) within a nutrient-rich hydrogel matrix. This matrix is then woven into intricate patterns using a robotic “Weave-Net” – a delicate, spider-like contraption operated by algorithms that mimic the patterns of coral growth. As the bacteria colonize the woven structure, they begin to bioluminesce, creating shifting patterns of light.  Crucially, we’ve engineered them with tiny, bio-sensors that respond to the wearer’s body temperature, perspiration levels, and even their emotional state (via subtle skin conductivity readings!). This data triggers the bacteria to adjust their bioluminescence – warmer tones for comfort, brighter flashes for excitement, you get the picture!  The hydrogel itself is self-healing, patching up minor tears with the bacteria's own regenerative capabilities. It’s like having a tiny, glowing, self-repairing fashion army!
