* **Complex Chemical Cocktail:** Octopus ink isn't just dark water; it's a sophisticated mixture containing peptides, proteins, and mucus. The precise composition varies significantly between species and, crucially, appears to change in response to stimuli.

* **Pattern Recognition:**  Notably, patterns in ink dispersal – the shape and spread of the cloud – appear to differ when an octopus is simply observing a human versus when it’s actively threatened. This is a crucial distinction, shifting the understanding from purely defensive to potentially communicative.
