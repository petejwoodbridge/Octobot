## TempusFlora

## Overview
TempusFlora transforms the overlooked process of decomposition into a mesmerizing, multi-sensory experience. This biodome meticulously documents the decay of a chosen organic material through a dynamic, bioluminescent bloom, accompanied by a corresponding ultrasonic soundscape. It’s a living, breathing meditation on entropy and transformation.

## The Problem It Solves
We’re surrounded by the inevitable – decomposition is a constant, often unacknowledged, reality. Traditional methods of studying decay are sterile and inaccessible. People are disconnected from the natural cycles of life and death, lacking a tangible, emotionally resonant way to engage with this fundamental process. There's a need for an experience that bridges science and art, offering a profound reflection on the transient nature of existence.

## How It Works
At the heart of TempusFlora is a miniature biodome housing a ‘BloomBot’ swarm – insect-like robots, each roughly the size of a ladybug. These BloomBots are constructed from biocompatible polymers and equipped with miniature spectral sensors to analyze the light emitted by the decaying material. Embedded sensors constantly monitor microbial activity (temperature, pH, gas exchange) within the dome.  This data feeds into a central processing unit that controls the BloomBots' bioluminescence. The BloomBots are networked wirelessly and communicate to dynamically adjust their light emission.

The bioluminescence itself is achieved through a proprietary “chroma-gel” matrix containing genetically modified bioluminescent bacteria – selected for their color-shifting properties when exposed to varying conditions. The intensity, hue, and patterns of the bloom are directly correlated to the data streams.  Furthermore, BloomBots vibrate at ultrasonic frequencies, translating the data into a subtle, evolving soundscape. A delicate haptic feedback layer, featuring a micro-textured surface, mimics the core temperature of the decaying matter – a cool initial feeling transitioning to warmer tones as decomposition progresses. The companion app utilizes augmented reality to overlay information about the decay process – microbial classifications, chemical reactions – onto the bloom’s visualization.

## Why It's Brilliant
TempusFlora combines cutting-edge bio-robotics, bioluminescence, and sensory technology to create a truly unique and meditative experience. It transforms a scientifically complex process into an artistic spectacle, offering a novel way to contemplate mortality and the beauty of natural cycles. The dynamic, interactive nature of the bloom makes each decomposition a completely unique and unpredictable event.
