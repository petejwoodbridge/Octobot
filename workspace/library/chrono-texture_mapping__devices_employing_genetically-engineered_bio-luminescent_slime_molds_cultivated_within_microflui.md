The current methods for assessing the age and condition of historical artifacts or even everyday objects – visual inspection, density scans, chemical analysis – are often indirect, subjective, and fail to provide a truly visceral understanding of their past. Furthermore, conservators and restorers struggle to accurately replicate the feel of original materials, limiting the effectiveness of restoration efforts. Chronoskin bridges this gap, offering a direct, intuitive connection to an object’s accumulated history.
