* **Spiritual ecology**: Thais believe that humans must maintain a harmonious relationship with nature and its spirits to ensure balance and well-being.
* **Folk medicine**: Shamans and traditional healers draw upon animistic principles to diagnose and treat illnesses, often incorporating rituals, prayers, and herbal remedies.

## Pragmatic Transactionalism
In Thai folk culture, pragmatic transactionalism refers to the art of negotiating with spirits, ancestors, and other supernatural entities to achieve desired outcomes. This involves:

* **Offerings**: Thais make offerings (e.g., food, incense, flowers) to appease spirits and ensure their cooperation.
* **Prayers**: Ritual prayers are recited to acknowledge the importance of these relationships and seek guidance or protection.
