*   **Focus (Beta Waves):** Higher levels of beta wave activity translate directly to increased acceleration, allowing players to build momentum with sustained concentration. The haptic feedback will be a gentle, pulsing vibration in the steering wheel.
*   **Excitement (Gamma Waves):** Sudden bursts of gamma wave activity trigger short, powerful speed boosts, simulating a rush of adrenaline. This will be accompanied by a sharp, punctuated vibration.
*   **Relaxation (Alpha Waves):** Maintaining a consistent alpha wave state activates defensive shields, providing a protective barrier against incoming obstacles. This will manifest as a steady, calming hum through the haptic feedback.
*   **Rhythmic Chaos:** If brainwave patterns become erratic – indicative of panic or loss of focus – the vehicle will violently destabilize, leading to crashes. The haptic feedback will escalate in intensity during periods of instability. The game will also feature dynamically generated track elements that react to the player's neural state – swirling vortexes for intense focus, or sudden jumps requiring quick reactions.
