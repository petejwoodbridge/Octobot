## ChromaBloom

## Overview
ChromaBloom is a bio-luminescent, genetically-engineered moss cultivated within a network of microfluidic channels embedded in a custom-shaped, 3D-printed sculpting tool. Artists can ‘paint’ with living light, creating transient, organic sculptures that shift color based on environmental stimuli and subtle emotional inputs from the artist, mirroring the aesthetics of light-emitting artists but with a dynamic, responsive element.

## The Problem It Solves
Traditional art mediums—paint, clay, metal—are static. They require constant manipulation and re-application, limiting the scope of expression and demanding significant physical effort. Furthermore, light-emitting artists often rely on external, controlled light sources, which can be expensive and restrictive in terms of color palette and dynamic control. This leaves a gap for truly immersive and responsive organic light art.

## How It Works
The ChromaBloom tool consists of a hand-held sculpting body crafted from a durable, bio-compatible polymer. Within this body are hundreds of tiny, interconnected microfluidic channels, each containing a carefully cultivated strain of *Physcomitrella patens* (a common moss) genetically modified to produce a range of bioluminescent colors. These channels are arranged in a complex, three-dimensional latticework, similar to a miniature vascular system. 

The artist manipulates the microfluidic system via a series of pressure-sensitive actuators embedded within the tool. Applying pressure to a channel controls the flow of a nutrient solution containing the moss spores and triggers bioluminescence, creating glowing lines and shapes. Crucially, sensors integrated into the tool monitor environmental factors—temperature, humidity, and ambient light—and subtly adjust the nutrient solution flow, causing the moss to shift color based on these changes. 

More powerfully, the tool incorporates biometrics: a small, non-invasive sensor detects subtle changes in the artist’s heart rate and skin conductance, translating these physiological data into commands that further modulate the nutrient flow and therefore, the color and intensity of the bioluminescence. The artist is, in essence, painting with their own bio-energy. This system has been inspired by the work of bio-artists who manipulate living organisms for artistic expression, and builds upon the chromatic control explored by light-emitting artists.

## Why It’s Brilliant
ChromaBloom offers an unparalleled level of control and responsiveness in light art. The dynamic color shifts, triggered by both external environment and the artist’s internal state, create mesmerizing, evolving sculptures. The use of a living, biological medium adds an element of unpredictability and organic beauty, blurring the line between art and life. It’s a conversation starter, a meditative tool, and a fundamentally new way to engage with light and form.
