Here’s where the ‘temporal cartography’ comes in. The sensors constantly analyze the vibrational patterns of microbial populations – essentially, listening to their ‘song’. Simultaneously, the micro-reservoirs release MycoGuard, providing a targeted burst of beneficial fungal support.
