## How It Works

The TTP is a roughly grapefruit-sized, organically-grown bio-resin sculpture. Within this sculpture resides a core of genetically modified *Luminospora symbiotica* – a newly discovered strain of bioluminescent fungi that responds directly to human biofeedback. Here’s the sequence:

1.  **Bio-Sync:** The user sits comfortably before the TTP, wearing a non-invasive EEG headband connected to the device. The headband monitors brainwave patterns, specifically identifying the neural signatures associated with a targeted traumatic memory.
2.  **Chromatic Mapping:** Based on the EEG data, the *Luminospora* shifts color in a specific, pre-programmed sequence – a “chromatic map” tailored to the user’s unique emotional response. These colors aren't random; they’ve been scientifically linked to specific emotional states (e.g., deep blue for sadness, vibrant orange for anger, calming green for fear).
3.  **Neuro-Acoustic Resonance:** Simultaneously, the TTP emits a stream of binaural beats and isochronic tones – precisely calibrated sound frequencies – delivered through miniature, bone-conduction transducers embedded within the sculpture. These tones are designed to resonate with the brainwaves identified by the EEG, subtly shifting the user’s state of consciousness.
4.  **Vibrational Sculpting:** Finally, the TTP generates micro-vibrations, controlled by an algorithm that analyzes the chromatic and acoustic data. These vibrations are incredibly subtle – below the threshold of conscious awareness – but they directly interact with the neural pathways associated with memory storage, gently ‘smoothing’ the edges of the traumatic experience. The bio-resin itself acts as a focal point for this therapeutic energy. 

The entire process lasts approximately 30 minutes, and the *Luminospora*’s luminescence gradually fades, symbolizing the diminishing intensity of the memory.
