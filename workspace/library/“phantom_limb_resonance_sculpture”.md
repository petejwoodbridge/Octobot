“The Resonance Bloom: A bio-responsive sculpture designed to address phantom limb pain and facilitate neurological remapping. Utilizing magnetoencephalography (MEG) to map the residual bio-magnetic fields associated with phantom sensations, the sculpture’s core – a complex network of shape-memory alloys, piezoelectric crystals, and bioluminescent microorganisms – translates these fields into a dynamic, three-dimensional form. The form *shifts* in response to the detected patterns, creating a constantly evolving ‘echo’ of the phantom limb. Simultaneously, the sculpture emits a personalized soundscape – generated via AI based on the user’s neurological data – designed to actively interfere with the phantom sensation and encourage the brain to reinterpret the area as ‘empty’.  Visual feedback is provided via micro-LED displays embedded within the sculpture, further reinforcing the spatial and sensory integration.  Think living, breathing art therapy… but way cooler! It’s… ink-scent-sational!”
