The intensity and frequency of the haptic feedback are directly linked to the emotional weight of the moment, as determined by the AI. The material itself subtly shifts color – a deep indigo for sorrow, a pulsing crimson for anger – reflecting the simulated emotional state.

Combining material science, biofeedback, and interactive art creates a profoundly resonant, multi-sensory experience. The system is incredibly adaptable, scaling from individual experiences to large-scale installations.
