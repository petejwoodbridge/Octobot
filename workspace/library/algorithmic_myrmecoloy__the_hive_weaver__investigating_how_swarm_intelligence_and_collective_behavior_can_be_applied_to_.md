3.  **Nutrient Cartridges:** Small, refillable nutrient cartridges, containing a custom blend of liquid fertilizer, are docked at the base of the pod. These cartridges contain compounds derived from seaweed and agricultural waste – promoting rapid decomposition and nutrient release.

The network *itself* adapts, sending more or less solution to specific areas based on the data, mirroring how a swarm of bees directs nectar flow.
5.  **Self-Regulation:** The mycelium naturally breaks down and regenerates, ensuring a constant, self-cleaning system.
