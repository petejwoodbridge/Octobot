## How It Works

The device is embedded with flexible, biocompatible OLEDs that emit carefully calibrated light patterns. More importantly, it’s coupled with a sophisticated biometric sensor array that continuously monitors the wearer’s heart rate variability (HRV), skin conductance, and cortisol levels.

When HRV deteriorates, indicating rising stress, the wristband’s AI – trained on the user’s personal memory database – initiates a pre-programmed sequence. The scent synthesizer generates a blend associated with a positive memory (e.g., a walk on the beach, a favorite meal, a loved one’s hug) while the OLEDs pulse with light mimicking the ambient conditions of that memory – warm, golden light for a beach, soft, blue light for a calm evening. The intensity and duration of the stimulation are dynamically adjusted based on the wearer’s biometric feedback, creating a truly personalized and responsive experience. The system is linked to a smartphone app where the user can log memories, rate their effectiveness, and train the AI. The humidity through micro-condensation technology built into the band maintains a comfortable skin temperature, optimizing scent delivery and light projection.
