The sphere, dubbed the ‘Chronosphere,’ is approximately the size of a grapefruit and constructed from a transparent, pressure-sensitive polymer. Embedded micro-actuators, guided by sophisticated biofeedback sensors, respond directly to the user’s movements – from the minute twitch of a finger to the full-body sway of a walk. \n\nThe gelatin itself is a proprietary blend of bio-polymers and luminescent algae, reacting to pressure and temperature changes to create a mesmerizing, shifting landscape of color and form.
