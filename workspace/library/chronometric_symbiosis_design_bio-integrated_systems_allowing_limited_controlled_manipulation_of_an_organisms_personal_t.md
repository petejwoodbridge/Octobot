Chronic wounds, particularly diabetic ulcers and severe burns, suffer from drastically prolonged healing times, often leading to amputations or significant disfigurement. Current therapies offer only symptomatic relief, not a fundamental shift in the rate at which the body or mind responds.

## How It Works

When the patch is activated (via a focused, low-level electromagnetic pulse – triggered by a discreet wristband), the tardigrades’ secretion dramatically slows the wearer's *local* perception of time within a 5cm radius.

Crucially, this isn’t actual time travel. Instead, the chronoproteins subtly alter neuronal firing rates, effectively lengthening the subjective ‘duration’ of an event as perceived by the wearer’s brain. For learning, the brain processes information at a slower, more deliberate pace, allowing for deeper consolidation and improved memory formation. A small reservoir of nutrient solution within the patch ensures the tardigrades' survival until the cycle ends.

Chronoskin uniquely combines the resilience and temporal manipulation capabilities of tardigrades with advanced bio-engineering. Furthermore, the system’s self-contained and cyclic operation minimizes risks associated with prolonged temporal alterations.
