### 2. The Sculpting Process - Iterative Refinement

The Neuro-Sculpting process isn't a one-shot deal. It’s a highly iterative process, drawing parallels with artistic sculpting:

1.  **Initial Seed:** The process begins with a basic design “seed” – a simple shape or layout.
2.  **ONN Generation:** The ONN generates multiple design variations based on the seed.
3.  **Human Feedback:** A human evaluator (often an architect or urban planner) provides feedback on each variation, indicating preferences – "more open," "less rigid," "incorporate natural light."
4.  **ONN Adjustment:** The ONN then adjusts its parameters to produce designs that are *more likely* to receive positive feedback, effectively “sculpting” the design over generations.
5.  **Repeat:** Steps 2-4 are repeated, leading to increasingly complex and refined designs.

* **Pattern:** The system tends to gravitate towards designs that possess inherent stability and efficiency – mirroring how biological systems evolve.
