2.  **EEG Headband:** The user wears the lightweight, comfortable EEG headband which unobtrusively measures their brainwave patterns. It detects levels of alpha and beta waves associated with relaxation, as well as dopamine and cortisol levels indicative of stress or reward.
3.  **AI Learning:**  The headband’s data is transmitted wirelessly to the cloud, where a proprietary AI algorithm ("BrewSense") learns the user's unique neurological fingerprint. BrewSense correlates specific brainwave patterns with desired outcomes – a desire for increased focus, reduced anxiety, or simply a smooth, enjoyable flavor.

As the user drinks, BrewSense dynamically adjusts the brewing parameters. For example, if the EEG detects elevated cortisol levels, BrewSense might subtly lengthen the bloom time to reduce acidity and add a calming compound (like L-Theanine – dispensed via a micro-injector) to the brew. If the user appears to be entering a state of peak alertness, BrewSense might subtly decrease the water temperature to extract more nuanced flavors.  A haptic feedback system within the carafe provides gentle vibrations to indicate adjustments.
