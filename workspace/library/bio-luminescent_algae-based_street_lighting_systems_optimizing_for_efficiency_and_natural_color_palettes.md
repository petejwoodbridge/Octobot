The fractal surface of the pods creates beautiful shimmering effects, transforming the street into a dynamic, living canvas of light. A central network connects the pods, monitoring their performance and intelligently distributing light based on real-time environmental data. The pods are also designed to passively ‘learn’ and adjust their chromatic output based on observed human behavior—dimming slightly during quiet hours and brightening during pedestrian traffic.
