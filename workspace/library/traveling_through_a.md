### What is a Wormhole?

A wormhole is a theoretical passage through space-time that could connect two distant points in the universe. The concept was first proposed by Albert Einstein's theory of general relativity, and has since been explored extensively in various fields including physics, mathematics, and science fiction.

### How Does it Work?

Wormholes are thought to be stabilized by a type of exotic matter that has negative energy density. This would allow for the creation of a "throat" or entrance to the wormhole, which would connect two points in space-time. Theoretically, if we could travel through a wormhole, it could reduce distances between two points in space-time, allowing for faster-than-light travel.
