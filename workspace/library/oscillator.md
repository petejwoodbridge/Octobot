## Overview
Resonant Bloom is a bio-acoustic pendant that translates subtle environmental vibrations—from traffic, machinery, even nearby conversations—into personalized, audible “bloom” patterns. These patterns, designed to be both aesthetically pleasing and subtly therapeutic, respond to the dominant frequencies around the wearer, creating a constantly shifting soundscape tailored to their immediate environment.

## The Problem It Solves
Modern life is a cacophony of noise, a relentless barrage of vibrational stimuli that contribute to stress, anxiety, and a general sense of unease. Current noise-canceling technology merely reduces sound, but doesn’t offer a proactive or calming response to the underlying vibrations impacting our well-being. People crave a more intuitive and engaging way to manage the constant, often unseen, sonic landscape.

## How It Works
The pendant itself houses a miniature, high-precision accelerometer combined with a custom-designed piezoelectric transducer. This accelerometer continuously monitors vibrations across a broad frequency range (20Hz - 20kHz). The data from the accelerometer is fed into a sophisticated micro-controller running a proprietary algorithm. This algorithm analyzes the vibration patterns, identifying dominant frequencies and their intensity. Based on this analysis, the piezoelectric transducer generates a series of precisely controlled, short-duration tones—the "blooms"—using a multi-channel speaker system.

These blooms aren’t random; they are mapped to a spectrum of auditory experiences: low-frequency blooms might be deep, pulsing bass, while high-frequency blooms resemble shimmering chimes. The intensity of the bloom directly corresponds to the intensity of the detected vibration. Crucially, the algorithm is personalized through a short calibration period; the wearer spends 5 minutes in a neutral space, and the pendant learns their baseline acoustic sensitivity. The pendant connects wirelessly to a companion app, allowing for customization of bloom palettes (e.g., “forest,” “city,” “meditation”), and tracking of vibrational exposure throughout the day.
