## How It Works
The Echo-Muse consists of three core components: a sensor array, a neural interface headset, and a haptic feedback suit. The algorithm also incorporates geological models and historical data to contextualize the events.

The visual component is generated by a micro-OLED array within the headset, projecting abstracted visualizations of geological formations and simulated plate movements based on the incoming seismic data. The experience is entirely reactive, constantly shifting and evolving based on the real-time geological activity.
