## How It Works
The Chronoscape Weave consists of a series of flexible, bio-adhesive patches approximately 2x2 inches. Each patch houses a miniature array of micro-LEDs capable of producing a vast spectrum of colors and dynamic lighting patterns. Beneath the LEDs are a network of micro-haptic actuators, incredibly small actuators that can generate subtle vibrations mimicking touch sensations – a gentle breeze, a firm handshake, or even the rumble of a distant explosion.

These patches are paired with a wearable sensor unit (worn discreetly around the wrist) that continuously monitors the wearer’s physiological data: heart rate variability (HRV), electroencephalogram (EEG) readings (brainwave patterns), and muscle tension via electromyography (EMG). This data feeds into a sophisticated AI engine that interprets the wearer’s emotional state – identifying levels of stress, anxiety, or relaxation.

Based on this analysis, the AI triggers the LEDs to generate the appropriate visual narrative (e.g., a calming blue forest scene) and simultaneously activates the haptic actuators to replicate corresponding sensations (e.g., the rustle of leaves, a cool breeze). As the wearer’s physiological state shifts (indicated by decreasing stress, for instance), the narrative dynamically adapts, offering moments of resolution and tranquility. Initial iterations will leverage AI collaboration, feeding user emotional response data into the AI to create truly unique, evolving stories.
