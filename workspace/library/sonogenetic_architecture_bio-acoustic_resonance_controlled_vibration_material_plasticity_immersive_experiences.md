Materials themselves subtly shift – a window pane might warp slightly, a wooden beam might develop a gentle, rhythmic undulation – enhancing the feeling of immersion. Different ‘Bloom Profiles’ – pre-programmed sonic and visual settings – can be selected to create calming meditative spaces, stimulating collaborative zones, or dramatic performance areas.
