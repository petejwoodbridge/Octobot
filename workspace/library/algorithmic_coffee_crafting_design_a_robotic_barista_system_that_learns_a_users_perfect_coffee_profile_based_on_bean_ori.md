## Overview
ChromaBrew is a robotic barista that doesn't just brew coffee; it *visualizes* your perfect cup. Using advanced spectrophotometry and a proprietary “Flavor-Mapping” algorithm, it analyzes the color of your coffee at every stage of the brewing process and translates that data into a dynamic, interactive color display reflecting the evolving flavor profile.

## The Problem It Solves
Current automated coffee machines offer limited customization and lack the ability to truly understand a user’s nuanced preferences. Consumers spend countless hours and dollars experimenting with different beans, settings, and brewing methods, often without a clear understanding of *why* one combination tastes better than another. This leads to frustration and wasted resources.

## How It Works
ChromaBrew utilizes a multi-stage brewing system housed within a sleek, cylindrical unit. First, the user selects a bean origin (from a database of over 5,000) and roast level via a holographic projection interface. Next, the system’s internal spectrometer analyzes the coffee’s color using a miniature, high-resolution spectrophotometer integrated into the portafilter. Simultaneously, the water temperature and flow rate are precisely controlled by a miniature Peltier system and micro-pump.

Crucially, the “Flavor-Mapping” algorithm, trained on a vast dataset of coffee flavor profiles linked to spectral data, predicts how these parameters will impact the resulting brew. The system then adjusts the brewing process *in real-time* based on the spectrometer readings – subtly altering water temperature, bloom time, and extraction pressure. 

As the coffee brews, the ChromaBrew projects a constantly shifting color gradient onto a touchscreen built into the base. The color’s hue, saturation, and brightness correspond directly to the perceived acidity, sweetness, body, and other flavor components.  A haptic feedback system integrated into the cup allows the user to "feel" the flavor complexity too. Finally, a digital log records all settings and sensory data, creating a personalized flavor profile for each user.

## Why It’s Brilliant
ChromaBrew bridges the gap between the scientific analysis of flavor and the subjective experience of tasting.
