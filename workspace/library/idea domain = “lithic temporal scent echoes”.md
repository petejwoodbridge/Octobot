When the piezoelectric actuator vibrates at the specific frequency derived from the memory recording, it triggers a cascade effect, causing the scent compounds to release in a carefully timed sequence, mirroring the sensory experience of the original moment. Finally, the stone’s surface is polished to a tactile perfection, providing a gentle haptic feedback loop synchronized with the scent release, further anchoring the memory.
