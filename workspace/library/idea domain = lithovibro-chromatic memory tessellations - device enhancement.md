## How It Works

The LCMCDE is built around a biocompatible, palm-sized device resembling a polished river stone – we’ve affectionately nicknamed them “Memory Stones.” Each stone houses a core of layered, nano-engineered piezoelectric crystals, meticulously arranged within a complex tessellation pattern. These crystals are the key! When the user focuses intently on a memory – utilizing a biofeedback headset that monitors brainwave patterns – the device begins to “listen” to the emotional signature of that memory. 

This isn’t just auditory or visual input; it’s a direct translation of neurological activity into vibrational energy. The piezoelectric crystals, sensitive to these subtle vibrations, begin to oscillate at corresponding frequencies. These oscillations, in turn, excite embedded, micro-LEDs within the tessellation, causing them to emit light of specific wavelengths – creating the chromatic patterns. 

Crucially, the tessellation itself *responds* to the user’s focus. As the user delves deeper into the memory, the patterns shift, evolve, and intensify, reflecting the nuanced layers of emotion. We've integrated a miniature, low-power quantum entanglement processor (patent pending!) to maintain a “memory signature” for each individual, ensuring consistent translation across different LCMCDE units.  Finally, haptic feedback – gentle vibrations – synchronize with the light patterns, further immersing the user in the experience.
