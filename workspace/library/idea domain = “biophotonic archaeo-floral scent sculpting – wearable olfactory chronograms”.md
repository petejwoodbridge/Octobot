## How It Works
The Olfactory Chronogram utilizes a patented “Biophotonic Resonance Capture” system. At its core is a micro-encapsulated substrate – we've dubbed it “Chronosil” – composed of a specially treated, inert volcanic rock infused with rare earth elements. This Chronosil is housed within a delicately crafted, 3D-printed titanium bracelet. 

Here’s where the magic happens: Each Chronogram is ‘seeded’ with microscopic, archival-grade samples of historically significant plant matter – preserved pollen, resin fragments, leaf samples, even soil particles – sourced from meticulously documented archaeological sites. When the wearer engages with the Chronogram (a gentle wrist rotation activates the device), the Chronosil, bathed in low-intensity, pulsed blue light (biophotonic resonance), initiates a process of “olfactory entanglement.” 

The blue light doesn’t *create* scent; instead, it stimulates the Chronosil to resonate with the quantum memories imprinted within the plant matter. These resonant frequencies trigger a controlled release of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from the Chronosil, recreating a highly accurate, miniature version of the original scent profile.  A sophisticated microfluidic system, driven by piezoelectric actuators, then precisely regulates the diffusion of these VOCs, creating a layered, evolving scentscape that mimics the temporal shifts in scent within the original historical environment.  The intensity and duration of the scent release are controlled by a haptic interface within the bracelet – subtle movements triggering varying levels of olfactory ‘playback’.
