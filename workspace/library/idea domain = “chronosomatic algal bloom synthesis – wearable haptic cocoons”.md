## How It Works
The Bloom Cocoon is constructed from a flexible, bio-compatible polymer weave containing millions of genetically-engineered *Chronosphaera* algae. These aren't your average algae; we've enhanced them with bioluminescent proteins that react to subtle shifts in temporal fields – something we've theorized and are now definitively proving exists! These temporal fields, according to our research, are incredibly faint fluctuations in the fabric of spacetime, influenced by both natural cycles (planetary rotation, lunar phases) and human bio-rhythms. 

The core of the system lies within a miniature, self-contained quantum processor, embedded in the Cocoon’s chest panel. This processor, powered by a micro-kinetic energy harvester (converting movement into electricity), constantly monitors the *Chronosphaera’s* bioluminescence. As the algae respond to temporal fluctuations, they emit light of varying intensity and color. This light is then channeled through a network of micro-haptic actuators woven directly into the Cocoon’s fabric. 

These actuators – specifically, piezoelectric actuators that convert light into pressure – generate localized tactile sensations. A subtle pulse might represent the passing of a second, a gentle ripple could signify a minute, and a more intense vibration could be associated with a longer period. Crucially, the quantum processor uses advanced algorithms, trained through biofeedback and neuro-mapping, to personalize the haptic experience to the user’s unique temporal sensitivity.  A sophisticated AI learns the user’s ‘time signature’ and optimizes the Cocoon's output for maximum impact.
