* **The Training Method:** The octopuses (specifically *Octopus vulgaris*, commonly known as the common octopus) were presented with a series of cards, each depicting an object or concept (e.g., “architecture,” “oceanography,” “music”) paired with a corresponding Dewey Decimal number (e.g., 724 for architecture, 500 for natural science). They were rewarded with fish for correctly associating the card with the number.
