The scanner meticulously records the lichen’s bioacoustic output over 24 hours, analyzing frequencies between 10Hz and 80Hz, specifically targeting the resonant frequencies observed during periods of environmental stress (desiccation, temperature fluctuations). The algorithm then dynamically adjusts the synthesized lichen resonance in real-time, subtly shifting frequencies and amplitudes based on the user's physiological response. For example, if HRV indicates stress, the system amplifies the frequencies associated with *Rhizocarpon’s* ‘stress-resistance’ signature. Finally, the system delivers the modulated resonance through bone conduction headphones, ensuring direct stimulation of the inner ear and bypassing the auditory cortex for maximum impact. The entire process is governed by a secure, cloud-based platform for data collection, personalization, and ongoing system optimization.
