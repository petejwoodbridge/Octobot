There’s a profound gap between objective data and the lived experience of emotion, and the Chrysalis is designed to bridge that chasm.

Instead, they're channeled through a ‘Lithic Matrix’ – a layered structure of bio-luminescent bacteria cultivated within a microfluidic chamber within the pendant. This matrix contains three distinct strains of bioluminescent bacteria, each genetically engineered to respond to a specific emotional state: *LuxSerene* (blue for calmness & tranquility), *LuxFranto* (amber for anxiety & stress), and *LuxJoy* (a vibrant, shifting spectrum for happiness & excitement). 

The ‘Lithic Echo Resonance’ technology utilizes precisely calibrated sonic pulses – delivered via bone conduction – to stimulate these bacterial colonies. The frequency and intensity of these pulses are modulated based on the real-time sensor data, creating a dynamic feedback loop. As your emotional state shifts, the bacterial colonies react, amplifying or diminishing the bioluminescence of the corresponding color, generating a mesmerizing, personalized light display that radiates outwards from the pendant. The intensity of the light is controlled by a miniature, biocompatible LED array within the pendant, providing a dynamic range of brilliance.
