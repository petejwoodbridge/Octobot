1. **Capture:** A subject – let’s call her Eleanor – enters the Memory Pod wearing a specially designed ‘Scent Weaver’ – a lightweight, bio-sensor garment interwoven with micro-pores. This Weaver constantly samples Eleanor’s immediate environment, collecting every scent particle – a pine needle, a specific leather, Eleanor’s own perfume – and transmitting the data to the LOP.

2. **Resonance & Crystallization:** The LOP’s crystals aren’t just passive collectors; they’re actively *learning*. Using a nano-scale ‘Olfactory Matrix,’ the crystals analyze the data and begin a process of ‘Olfactory Resonance.’ This isn’t just duplication; it’s an *interpretation* of the scent, replicating its chemical signature with astonishing accuracy. The crystals slowly grow, incorporating the recorded scents into their structure.

3. **Playback:** To experience the Palimpsest, another person enters a dedicated ‘Receptor Chamber’ – a spherical space lined with micro-sensors. The LOP, now fully crystallized, releases a precisely calibrated stream of scent into the Chamber, recreating the original olfactory experience. The intensity and complexity of the scent are dynamically adjusted based on the ‘Receptor’s’ neurological response, creating a truly personalized and immersive recall.
