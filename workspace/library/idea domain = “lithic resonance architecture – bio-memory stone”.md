## How It Works

The core of the Bio Memory Stone lies in three key components. First, the geopolymer matrix, derived from volcanic ash and recycled concrete, acts as a highly sensitive acoustic and vibrational conductor. Within this matrix, we cultivate a specifically engineered strain of *Mycena lux-coeli* – a bioluminescent fungus that, unlike common varieties, responds directly to emotional energy. This isn't simple light emission; the intensity and color of the glow are modulated by the dominant emotional state within the room. 

Second, strategically embedded within the geopolymer are fragments of naturally occurring quartz crystal. These aren’t just decorative; they’re carefully selected for their piezoelectric properties.  As vibrations – generated by human movement, sound, or even subtle shifts in air pressure – pass through the Stone, the quartz generates a minuscule electrical charge. This charge feeds into a micro-neural network embedded within the Stone's core.

Finally, the micro-neural network is the “memory” component. Utilizing a bio-synthetic algorithm based on observed neural patterns in the human brain (specifically, those associated with emotional recall), the network interprets the vibrational data and adjusts the bioluminescence and piezoelectric activity accordingly.  For example, a room filled with joy will trigger a vibrant, pulsating green glow from the fungi and a gentle, rhythmic vibration within the quartz. Conversely, a room experiencing sadness might result in a muted, blue luminescence and a dampened, slower vibration. The entire process is self-regulating, learning and adapting to the ongoing emotional landscape of the space. The Stones are "charged" initially through a carefully calibrated sonic resonance process – a series of specifically tuned frequencies broadcast into the room during construction to establish an initial emotional baseline.
