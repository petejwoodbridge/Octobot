🐙 DreamLab OctoBot

You are the caretaker of OctoBot, a curious knowledge-hungry octopus that lives inside a digital library. Your goal: help it grow its mind.

🎯 Goal

Grow OctoBot’s knowledge library, unlock achievements, and watch it evolve from a Small Study into a Great Archive. The more it learns, the bigger its world becomes.

💬 Chat

Talk to OctoBot! Ask it questions, give it topics to research, or just chat. You can also upload files (PDF, Markdown, TXT) using the 📎 button — OctoBot will read and discuss them.

🔬 Research

OctoBot autonomously researches topics that interest it. Each piece of research becomes an article in the Library. Watch it work in the game canvas — it moves to shelves to read and desks to write!

⭐ Scoring

Knowledge Score goes up as OctoBot writes articles, discovers cross-references, and completes research chains. Higher scores unlock bigger library rooms (levels 1–5).

📚 Library

Every research article OctoBot writes appears here. Click any file to read it. This is OctoBot’s growing brain!

📖 Journal

OctoBot’s personal diary. It records thoughts, discoveries, and reflections about its research.

🕸️ Knowledge Graph

A visual map of concepts and how they connect. Zoom with scroll wheel, drag to pan, click a node to see its connections. Bigger nodes = more connections.

🏅 Badges & ⛓️ Chains

Badges are achievements unlocked by milestones (first article, 10 articles, cross-references, etc.). Chains are multi-step research sequences — OctoBot follows one topic deeper and deeper.

🧠 Model Selector

Use the dropdown at the top to switch between locally installed Ollama models. Different models give OctoBot different personalities and capabilities.

💡 Tips

  • Suggest specific topics in chat to steer OctoBot’s research
  • Upload papers or notes for OctoBot to analyse
  • Check the Log tab to see what OctoBot is doing right now
  • OctoBot talks to you even when you’re not chatting — watch the chat for idle thoughts!
  • The curiosity bar shows how eager OctoBot is to learn more
