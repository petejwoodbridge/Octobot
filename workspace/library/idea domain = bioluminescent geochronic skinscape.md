These algae respond directly to Chronos’ data, shifting color, intensity, and form based on the emotional valence of the targeted memory. The intensity of the bloom is proportional to the emotional strength associated with the memory. Finally, a haptic feedback system subtly vibrates the suit in sync with the light patterns, creating a fully immersive sensory experience.
