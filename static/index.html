🐙 DreamLab OctoBot — Idea Machine

You are the co-inventor alongside OctoBot, a gloriously chaotic pink octopus idea machine. Your goal: generate as many brilliant, original, never-before-invented ideas as possible.

🎯 Goal

Help OctoBot generate as many brilliant, original ideas as possible. Watch it evolve from a Napkin Sketcher into a Visionary Inventor. The more it invents, the bigger its world becomes!

💬 Chat

Talk to OctoBot! Suggest problems to solve, pitch ideas together, or just chat. OctoBot DROPS EVERYTHING when you message it. You can also upload files (PDF, Markdown, TXT) using the 📎 button for inspiration.

🔬 Idea Generation

OctoBot autonomously generates ideas for products, gadgets, services, creative projects, and solutions. Each idea is saved to the Idea Vault. Watch it work — it moves to desks to sketch and shelves to review past ideas!

⭐ Scoring

Idea Score goes up as OctoBot generates ideas, fuses concepts, and completes idea chains. Higher scores unlock 20 inventor levels — from Napkin Sketcher to Infinite Idea Engine.

💡 Idea Vault

Every idea OctoBot generates appears here. Click any idea to read the full pitch. This is OctoBot’s creative brain!

📖 Journal

OctoBot’s inventor diary. It records eureka moments, idea fusions, and wild observations about humans and nature.

🕸️ Idea Graph

A visual map of concepts and how they connect across ideas. Zoom with scroll wheel, drag to pan, click a node to see connections. Bigger nodes = more idea connections.

🏅 Badges & ⛓️ Chains

Badges are achievements unlocked by milestones (first idea, 10 ideas, idea fusions, etc.). Chains are multi-step invention sequences — OctoBot follows one problem domain deeper and wilder.

🧠 Model Selector

Use the dropdown at the top to switch between locally installed Ollama models. Different models give OctoBot different creative styles.

🌟 Tips

  • Suggest problems or domains in chat to steer OctoBot’s inventions
  • Upload articles or notes as inspiration fuel
  • Check the Log tab to see what OctoBot is inventing right now
  • OctoBot talks even when you’re not chatting — watch for random idea bursts!
  • The creativity bar shows how fired up OctoBot is to invent
