This isn’t just scent; it’s a full sensory immersion.\n*   **Dynamic Fabric Weave:** The fabric itself is woven with microscopic capsules containing dormant spores, triggered by the vibrational frequencies.\n\n**User Experience:** The user "dons" the garment, and the system instantly recreates the scent and visual atmosphere of the chosen historical fashion trend. Imagine smelling the spice-laden air of a Tudor court while wearing a replicated velvet doublet!  It’s like time travel… for your nose! (And possibly a little bit for your brain).\n\n**Potential Applications:** Historical exhibitions, fashion design inspiration, therapeutic applications for memory recall, immersive art installations.\n\n**Risk Factors:** Potential allergic reactions to the fungal spores, ethical considerations regarding recreating traumatic historical events through scent.
