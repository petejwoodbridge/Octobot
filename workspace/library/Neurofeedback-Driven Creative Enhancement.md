“Project: JellyPulse - A neurofeedback device that translates brainwave patterns into bioluminescent jellyfish-like displays. The device monitors the user’s cognitive state (focus, relaxation, excitement) and generates corresponding patterns of light and movement using a miniature, contained bioluminescent culture of *Aequorea victoria* (the jellyfish that makes GFP – green fluorescent protein!). The jellyfish are housed in a flexible, pressure-sensitive chamber, creating a mesmerizing, pulsing display that reacts to the user’s thoughts. Advanced haptic feedback stimulates the user’s skin, replicating the gentle touch of the jellyfish. It’s like dancing with your mind! Seriously, this could unlock HUGE creative potential! Plus, the jellyfish are *adorable*!”
