“The Bloom: A Decentralized Philanthropic Sculpture.  The Bloom is a dynamic, modular sculpture constructed from reclaimed materials, physically representing the impact of donations to verified, blockchain-tracked charities. Users donate to specific projects listed on a decentralized app – each project is linked to a ‘node’ within the sculpture’s design. As donations accumulate, that node grows, changes shape, and incorporates new materials (recycled plastic, bioplastics, even cultivated mycelium!) – visually demonstrating the growing impact.  The sculpture is built by a global network of 'Bloom Artisans’ - skilled makers commissioned to physically realize the sculpture's design.  Transparency is key – all transactions, material sourcing, and artistic decisions are recorded on the blockchain.  Rewards include digital badges, recognition within the Bloom community, and even the opportunity to design a node themselves!  Think: charity, art, fabrication, blockchain… it’s a whole ecosystem of ink-credible goodness!”
