The foundation of this research lies in the incredibly complex and dynamic communication system of cuttlefish. * **Chromatophores:** These pigment-containing cells respond to neural signals, allowing for incredibly fast and complex colour patterns.
* **Bioluminescence:** Cuttlefish produce light through a chemical reaction involving luciferin and luciferase. Crucially, they control *both* the intensity and duration of these flashes.
