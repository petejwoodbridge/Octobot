## The Problem It Solves
Currently, understanding the rate and processes of decomposition is largely qualitative and dependent on expert observation – a time-consuming and inaccurate process. Forensic investigations, archaeological studies, and even simple gardening benefit from precise data on how quickly materials degrade, but existing methods are limited by their reliance on subjective interpretation and lack of quantifiable metrics. Chronoscent fills this gap by providing an objective, immersive, and instantly-generated map of the decomposition timeline.

This unit processes the data, running an algorithm that simulates the microbial activity and enzymatic reactions driving the decomposition process.
