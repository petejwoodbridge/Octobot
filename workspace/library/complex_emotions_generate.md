We often experience a flood of sadness, anger, or anxiety, but lack the tools to dissect it, identify the triggering factors, and gently guide ourselves back to equilibrium. Current methods like journaling and meditation are often passive and require significant self-discipline.

## How It Works

These sensors continuously monitor subtle physiological changes associated with emotional states: heart rate variability, skin conductivity, pupil dilation, and even micro-facial muscle movements.

But here’s the key: the Lumina Heart doesn't *detect* the emotion. Instead, it *responds* to the immediate neural signature of overwhelming feeling. When a significant spike in stress-related activity is detected, the pendant initiates a personalized “Lumina Sequence.” This sequence involves precisely calibrated pulses of colored light emitted from the micro-LEDs – each color represents a different aspect of emotional regulation. 

For example, a dominant red signal signifies intense anger, triggering a slow, rhythmic pulsing sequence designed to promote relaxation. A blue pulse indicates sadness, prompting a gentle, wave-like light pattern associated with introspection and self-compassion. The sequences are pre-programmed based on extensive research into the neurobiology of emotion and continuously learn the user's unique response patterns over time. Data is encrypted and relayed to a secure app for analysis and personalized recommendations.
