## Habitats
Trenches can be divided into three main habitats:

* **Abyssal plains**: Flat or gently sloping areas with minimal topography and soft sediments.
* **Seamounts**: Underwater volcanoes that can create unique ecosystems through hydrothermal activity.
* **Hydrothermal vents**: Areas where hot fluids from the Earth's interior mix with cold seawater, creating chemical-rich environments.

## Adaptations
Organisms in trenches have developed remarkable adaptations to cope with the extreme conditions:

* **Bioluminescence**: Many species produce light to communicate, attract prey, or defend against predators.
* **Large eyes**: Some fish have massive eyes to detect bioluminescent prey in the dark.
* **Highly efficient respiration**: Organisms have evolved to conserve energy and oxygen due to the limited availability of these resources.
* **Slow metabolism**: Many species have slow metabolisms to conserve energy, as food is scarce.
