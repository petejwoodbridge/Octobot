“Project: ChitterSync - A biofeedback system utilizing miniature, drone-controlled chickens to monitor and subtly influence a user’s stress levels. Each chicken is equipped with haptic sensors and a tiny speaker emitting soothing sounds or, if needed, gentle vibrations tailored to the user's physiological response. The drones, programmed with AI, learn the user’s unique ‘chick-tural’ response patterns. Think of it as a feathered, flapping anxiety antidote!”
