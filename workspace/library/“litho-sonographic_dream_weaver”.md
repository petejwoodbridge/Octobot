**How It Works**

Here’s the delightfully complicated bit. The dreamer wears a comfortable, sensor-laden headband that continuously monitors their brainwaves, focusing on the alpha and theta frequencies associated with REM sleep. This data is fed into the core of the device – a miniature, bio-resonant synthesizer. 

The synthesizer then generates a complex, constantly shifting sonic pattern, tailored to the dreamer's unique neural signature. This sound then vibrates the Somnium Fluid, causing the pigments to coalesce and solidify into a miniature, geologically accurate representation of the dream. The entire process is overseen by an AI named "Percival," who occasionally offers insightful (and wildly inaccurate) commentary about the dream formations. Percival has a penchant for quoting Shakespeare.
