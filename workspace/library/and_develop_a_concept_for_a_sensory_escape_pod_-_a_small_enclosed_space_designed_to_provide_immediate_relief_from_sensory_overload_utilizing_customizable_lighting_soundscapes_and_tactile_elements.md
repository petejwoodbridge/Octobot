Sensory overload – the crushing wave of noise, light, and tactile stimulation – is a surprisingly common and debilitating condition. Existing solutions, like noise-canceling headphones or darkened rooms, are often inadequate. They treat the *symptoms* but not the root cause: the overwhelming influx of raw sensory data. People experiencing this need a precise, calibrated reset, a way to immediately withdraw and recalibrate their nervous system.

Micro-vibrators, arranged in a geometric pattern, deliver subtle, programmable pulses of pressure. 4. **Atmospheric Control:** A miniature humidifier allows for a subtle, calming mist, and the pod maintains a constant, perfectly-regulated temperature.
