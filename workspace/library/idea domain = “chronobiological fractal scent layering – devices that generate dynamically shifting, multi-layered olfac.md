## How It Works
Each Echo Bloom unit is a palm-sized, organically shaped device crafted from bio-resin infused with bioluminescent algae for a subtle, ambient glow. At its core is the “Fractal Core,” a micro-fabricated device containing thousands of tiny, independently controlled micro-diffusers. These diffusers aren’t filled with essential oils in the traditional sense; instead, they house meticulously formulated ‘Scent Pods’ – tiny, gel-based cartridges containing a blend of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) precisely tuned to trigger specific neurological responses. 

Here's the clever part: The Fractal Core utilizes a complex algorithm, driven by a miniature, bio-integrated chronometer (using a genetically engineered strain of *Vibrio fischeri* – the light-emitting bacteria – to measure time changes in light levels) and a biometric sensor array (measuring skin temperature, heart rate variability, and even subtle perspiration analysis) to dynamically adjust the release rates and spatial distribution of the Scent Pods. 

The fractal geometry isn’t just for aesthetics; it’s critical. The micro-diffusers are arranged in a self-similar fractal pattern, creating a complex, multi-layered diffusion field. This allows for multiple scent “layers” to be released simultaneously, blending and shifting over time, mimicking the way our own scent glands produce complex scent mixtures. Think of it like a scent orchestra, with each micro-diffuser playing a distinct note within a beautiful, evolving composition. A built-in haptic feedback system subtly adjusts the device's temperature to further modulate scent release based on biometric input.
