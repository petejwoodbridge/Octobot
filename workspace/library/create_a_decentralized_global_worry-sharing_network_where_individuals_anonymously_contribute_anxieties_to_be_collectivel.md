## The Problem It Solves
Modern anxiety is often isolating and unspoken. While therapy and support groups exist, accessing them can be difficult and stigmatizing. Furthermore, experiencing anxiety feels intensely personal, often leaving individuals with no external validation or understanding of the sheer scale of the problem. Existing art forms rarely capture the raw, visceral nature of anxiety in a way that fosters connection.
