## What Are These?
The word "these" is an indefinite article, used to refer to nouns that are singular or plural. It indicates a non-specific quantity or no specific quantity at all! For example:

* I'll take these books from the shelf.
* These new shoes are really comfortable.
In both cases, "these" is used to refer to a group of items without specifying the exact number.
