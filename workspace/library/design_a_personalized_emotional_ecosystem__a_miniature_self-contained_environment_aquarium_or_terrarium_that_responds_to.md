This data feeds into a miniature, low-power AI chip – a custom-built 'CalmCore' – that’s linked to a smart home hub via Bluetooth. The CalmCore analyzes the biometric data and triggers a series of automated adjustments: 
1. **Acoustic Optimization:** Based on HRV analysis, the system adjusts the internal soundscape. Elevated GSR triggers a slow, layered ambient soundscape of binaural beats and white noise, while low GSR triggers a gentle, nature-inspired soundscape.
2. **Light Modulation:** The bioluminescent microorganisms react to specific light frequencies.  High stress levels activate a pulsing, deep blue light, mimicking the calming effect of the night sky. Lower stress levels shift the light to warmer, amber tones.
3. **Hydrodynamic Response:** A miniature, silent pump regulates the water flow within the terrarium. During periods of elevated stress, the pump creates gentle, swirling currents, mimicking the feeling of flowing water – a proven stress-reducing stimulus. The water itself is filtered with bio-char to absorb excess adrenaline and cortisol metabolites from the user’s hand contact.
