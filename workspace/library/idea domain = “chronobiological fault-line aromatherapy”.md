## How It Works

The Chronobiological Fault Line employs a multi-layered system centered around the “Chrono-Scent” cartridges. Each cartridge contains a meticulously crafted blend of essential oils, terpene profiles, and subtle ultrasonic vibrations, specifically designed to stimulate or suppress olfactory receptors at precise moments in the day. 

Here's the breakdown: 

1.  **Biofeedback Sensor Array:** The device sits discreetly on a bedside table and incorporates a miniature, non-invasive biofeedback sensor array. This array monitors several key physiological markers in real-time: heart rate variability (HRV), skin temperature, and subtle muscle tension. 

2.  **Temporal Mapping Algorithm:**  Data from the sensor array feeds into a sophisticated AI algorithm – affectionately nicknamed “Kairos” – which is constantly mapping the user’s unique circadian rhythm based on their individual biometrics.  Kairos doesn’t just track; it *learns* your rhythms with astonishing accuracy. 

3.  **Chrono-Scent Delivery:** Based on Kairos’s analysis, the device selects the appropriate Chrono-Scent cartridge and activates its ultrasonic emitter. These emitters don't simply diffuse the scent; they create a localized, precisely-timed “scent wave” that interacts directly with the olfactory bulb.  For example, if the sensor detects a dip in HRV indicating fatigue just before your typical evening slump, Kairos will trigger a blend of rosemary and peppermint – known for their stimulating effects – delivered in a 15-second burst. 

4.  **Dynamic Adjustment:**  Crucially, the Fault Line *adapts*.  As the user's rhythm shifts throughout the day (due to light exposure, activity, or even caffeine intake), Kairos continuously recalibrates the scent delivery, creating a dynamic, responsive olfactory environment.
