*   **Variational Autoencoders (VAEs):** VAEs, used for generating data, are showing promise in modelling the uncertainty inherent in mindfulness – representing data with a probabilistic distribution rather than a fixed point.
*   **Bayesian Neural Networks:** These networks incorporate prior beliefs about the data, reflecting the meditator’s willingness to suspend judgment and consider multiple perspectives.
