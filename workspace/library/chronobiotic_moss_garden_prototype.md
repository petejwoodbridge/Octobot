“Project: ChronoBloom – A bio-acoustic moss garden. Core Concept: Genetically engineered moss colonies, cultivated within dynamically controlled micro-environments mirroring historical periods. Data Streams: Moss growth rate (measured via nano-sensors), ambient temperature, humidity, light levels, and – crucially – vibrational data from the moss itself. This data is processed via a biofeedback algorithm and translated into a unique musical composition in real-time. User Interface: A haptic glove provides tactile feedback – simulating the moss’s texture and subtle movements. Olfactory output – synthesized scent profiles mirroring the historical period's dominant flora. Goal: To create an immersive, synesthetic experience allowing users to ‘listen’ and ‘feel’ the past through the sensory echoes of the moss. Initial Focus: Cambrian Period – replicating the atmosphere of a primordial swamp. Potential Partnerships: Bio-engineers specializing in moss manipulation, sound artists, perfumers, historical ecology experts.”
