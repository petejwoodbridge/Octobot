### Background: The Problem of Parchment Assessment

Current methods often categorize damage into broad levels (e.g., ‘Good’, ‘Fair’, ‘Poor’) without providing nuanced understanding or predictive capability. This poses a major hurdle in accurately determining the conservation needs and historical significance of fragile parchment artifacts.
