## The Problem It Solves
The agonizing persistence of traumatic memories – the flashbacks, the anxiety, the debilitating feelings – is a global health crisis. Current therapies often focus on coping mechanisms, but rarely address the root of the emotional distress embedded within the memory itself. Existing “dissolve sensory memories” technologies tend to flatten experiences, losing detail and emotional context. Chronosync seeks to restore the *feeling* of the memory, not eliminate it.

Advanced AI monitors the user's physiological response (heart rate variability, skin conductance) and adjusts the sensory weave in real-time, ensuring optimal emotional impact.
