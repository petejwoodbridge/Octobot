## The Problem It Solves
The human experience of time is notoriously subjective and easily distorted. We often struggle to maintain a sense of perspective, leading to feelings of boredom, anxiety, or a distorted perception of how quickly time passes. Current methods of tracking time—watches, calendars—are passive and often fail to connect with our most immediate, internal experience. There's a lack of tangible, personal tools to help us consciously manage our relationship with time.

## How It Works
ChronoWeave utilizes a complex, interwoven system. The core of the invention is a microfluidic matrix cultivated with *Mycena luxaeterna*, a naturally bioluminescent fungus known for its exceptionally long lifespan and intensity. This matrix is integrated into a bio-compatible, self-healing wool yarn, creating a durable textile. Woven into the yarn are subcutaneous biofeedback sensors that continuously monitor the wearer's physiological rhythms: heart rate variability, skin temperature fluctuations, and cortisol levels—key indicators of stress and time perception. This data is streamed wirelessly to a generative algorithm housed within a miniature, flexible circuit board sewn into the garment’s lining.

The algorithm interprets this data in real-time, adjusting the bioluminescence of the fungal colonies. The intensity and colour of the light are dynamically controlled based on the wearer's physiological state – calming blues for reduced cortisol, pulsating greens for increased heart rate variability, and shifting oranges for moments of heightened awareness. Simultaneously, controlled spore dispersal is triggered, releasing microscopic spores engineered to subtly influence the wearer’s subjective experience of time. These spores, while entirely harmless, contain a targeted peptide sequence that interacts with the body’s internal circadian rhythms, momentarily altering perceived temporal duration.

The ‘Tempo’ companion app allows users to manually influence the fungal embroidery, providing further control and exploration of the chronographic tapestry.

The self-healing yarn ensures longevity, and the spore technology introduces a genuinely novel element – a direct, albeit gentle, influence on how we *feel* time passing.
