## Overview
The NeuroSync Stabilizer is a wearable exoskeletal brace seamlessly integrated with biofeedback sensors, dynamically adjusting its support based on the wearer’s subconscious movements and balance adjustments. It’s not just about preventing falls; it’s about learning and reinforcing optimal movement patterns for stability.

## The Problem It Solves
Current exoskeletons are often rigid and require conscious effort to control, creating a disconnect between the user and their body. This can lead to reliance on the device and hinder natural movement recovery after injury or neurological events like stroke or Parkinson's. Furthermore, existing systems don't actively *teach* the body how to stabilize itself.
