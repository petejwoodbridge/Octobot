## How It Works

The magic, my friends, lies within the Cartridge itself. Each unit is a meticulously crafted, palm-sized sphere constructed from a layered composite of resonant quartz, stabilized bismuth alloy, and a proprietary “Temporal Shard” – a synthesized crystalline matrix capable of generating localized gravitational anomalies. Activation occurs via a handheld Resonance Amplifier – a sleek, ergonomically designed device emitting a precisely calibrated frequency. 

When the Amplifier is aimed at the Cartridge, it triggers a cascade within the Temporal Shard. This, in turn, generates a localized distortion field – roughly 1-meter in diameter – affecting only the immediate vicinity. Within this field, the user experiences a carefully choreographed sensory illusion. We've developed three core “Epochs” – each triggered by a different frequency:

*   **Epoch Echo:** Projects a fleeting auditory hallucination – a snippet of music from the 1920s, the clang of a Victorian blacksmith’s hammer, or the distant chatter of a Roman marketplace.
*   **Epoch Bloom:** Creates a transient visual distortion – a momentary shift in color, a fleeting projection of a historical image (a dinosaur, a Victorian tea party, a futuristic cityscape), or the sensation of floating through a dense fog.
*   **Epoch Ripple:** Generates a subtle tactile anomaly – a brief sensation of cold, warmth, or a gentle vibration, mimicking the feeling of a passing breeze or a shifting tectonic plate.

The entire process lasts approximately 10-15 seconds, creating a genuinely surprising and memorable experience. Data is streamed wirelessly from the Resonance Amplifier to the Cartridge via a near-field communication protocol, ensuring consistent and reliable operation.
