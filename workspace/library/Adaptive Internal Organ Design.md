## How It Works

Inside, a network of microfluidic channels houses genetically engineered bioluminescent algae colonies. Calibration occurs through a “Chromatic Resonance” session. This involves a personalized guided meditation session – delivered through bone conduction headphones – combined with haptic feedback delivered through the garment. A companion app streams this data in real-time, allowing for monitoring and personalized biofeedback training programs.
