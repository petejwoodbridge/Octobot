## ChronoBloom

**The Idea:** ChronoBloom is a personalized, scent-based time-management system embedded within a minimalist, ceramic hourglass. 

## The Problem It Solves: Scattered Focus & Task Inertia

We’ve all experienced it: staring at a to-do list, feeling overwhelmed, and then just…doing nothing. The modern world bombards us with notifications and distractions, making it incredibly difficult to maintain sustained focus. Traditional timers are jarring and often ineffective. ChronoBloom tackles this by directly addressing the neurological impact of time – our inherent connection to scent and memory. It combats task inertia by associating specific scents with scheduled activities.

## How It Works: Bio-Scent Sequencing

The hourglass itself houses a tiny, refillable reservoir containing four precisely calibrated, organic essential oil blends. Each blend is linked to a specific timeframe (e.g., ‘Focus Flow’ - rosemary and lemon for 30 minutes, ‘Creative Spark’ - bergamot and ylang-ylang for 60 minutes, ‘Rest & Reflect’ – lavender and chamomile for 15 minutes, ‘Energy Burst’ - grapefruit and peppermint for 90 minutes). 

A miniature, biofeedback sensor (integrated into the base) subtly monitors the user’s heart rate variability (HRV) – a key indicator of stress and cognitive load.  When the hourglass reaches its halfway point, the sensor analyzes HRV. If stress levels are elevated (indicating difficulty focusing), it triggers the release of the ‘Focus Flow’ scent. This repeated association – time passing, scent release, improved HRV – conditions the brain to naturally associate the scent with optimal productivity and focus. The user simply chooses a timeframe and the hourglass does the rest.
