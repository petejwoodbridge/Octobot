### Mindful Eating 101
Mindful eating involves paying attention to your physical sensations while consuming food. It's not about restriction or perfection; rather, it's an invitation to savor the experience. When done correctly, mindful eating can:

* Slow down eating pace, reducing digestive discomfort
* Increase enjoyment of food, improving appetite and satisfaction
* Foster a sense of calm and connection with one's body

### Research: Mindful Eating for Stress Reduction

* Activate the parasympathetic nervous system (rest and digest), counteracting stress
* Decrease cortisol levels, a hormone associated with stress
