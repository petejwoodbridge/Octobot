Furthermore, the ephemeral nature of scent – lost to erosion, weathering, and human intervention – represents a massive loss of historical data. Existing archaeological surveys are incredibly reliant on surface finds, ignoring the rich, complex sensory information locked within the ground beneath our feet. The LTVOG directly addresses this by providing a truly holistic and immediate sensory reconstruction of a site's geological past.
