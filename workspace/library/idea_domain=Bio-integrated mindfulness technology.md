Furthermore, existing mindfulness techniques often rely on external prompts – guided meditations, calming music – which can feel forced and ultimately, ineffective when the mind is truly overwhelmed. There’s a critical need for a personalized, responsive solution that doesn’t just mask disturbance but actively addresses the root causes of stress and promotes genuine inner peace.
