## The Problem It Solves

The current state of bioluminescent art installations and educational displays is static – beautiful, yes, but ultimately unchanging. Observing microbial bioluminescence, particularly within carefully cultivated cultures, requires incredibly dark environments, specialized equipment, and a relatively short observation window. Understanding the nuances of microbial behavior within these systems – their metabolic shifts, the impact of environmental changes – is exceptionally difficult. We need a way to *listen* to and *visualize* the bioluminescent conversation happening inside these tiny, glowing worlds.

## How It Works

This shift dramatically increases the intensity and color profile of their bioluminescence.

The vessels themselves are designed with varying levels of porosity, creating a tiered system of observation, mimicking natural environments.
