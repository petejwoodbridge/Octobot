## The Problem It Solves

Grief is profoundly isolating. Traditional memorials, while valuable, often feel static and distant, failing to truly connect visitors with the memory of the deceased. Existing AR memorials can be visually impressive but lack a deeply personal, emotionally resonant layer. Current biofeedback technologies are primarily used for fitness, not for facilitating profound emotional processing. Echo Bloom addresses all three, offering a memorial experience designed to foster genuine connection and facilitate a more authentic, individual grieving process.

## How It Works

Echo Bloom is a personalized AR memorial delivered through a lightweight, haptic wristband and a small, palm-sized “Bloom” device – a beautifully sculpted, ceramic flower that sits on a designated memorial table. 

Here’s the breakdown:

1.  **Personalized Resonance:** Before visiting, the user (or their family) uploads a collection of data: short audio recordings of the deceased speaking, photos with associated memories, and specific, deeply personal facts – their favorite color, a childhood joke, a dream they held.
2.  **Biofeedback Trigger:** As the user approaches the Bloom device, their wristband – equipped with a heart rate variability (HRV) sensor and skin conductance sensor – begins passively monitoring their stress levels.
3.  **AR Bloom Activation:**  When the user’s HRV indicates heightened stress (typically correlating with moments of intense emotion), the Bloom device springs to life. It projects an AR representation of the deceased – not a perfect likeness, but an impression of their energy, drawn from the uploaded data – into the space around the flower. 
4.  **Sensory Echoes:** The projection is coupled with subtle haptic feedback from the wristband – gentle pulses mimicking the deceased's heartbeat, or the warmth of a remembered hug.  Soundscapes are generated using the uploaded audio, subtly layered and responsive to the user's biofeedback. For example, if the user displays anxiety, the soundscape might shift to a calmer, more nostalgic melody. 
5.  **Dynamic Reflection:**  The AR projection isn't static; it subtly “blooms” and fades based on the user's emotional responses, creating a dynamic reflection of their grief and remembrance.
