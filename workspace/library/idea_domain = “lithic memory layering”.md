## How It Works
The Chronoscent Stone operates through a multi-stage process. First, a miniaturized, self-contained geological weathering model – roughly the size of a palm – is activated. This model simulates the processes of erosion, freeze-thaw cycles, and chemical weathering that would have affected the object over geological timescales. Simultaneously, a highly sensitive laser scanner maps the micro-fractures, polish patterns, and overall surface irregularities of the stone tool held against the device.

This data is then fed into a bio-reconstructed organic compound synthesizer. This synthesizer uses a proprietary algorithm to translate the scanned data into a precise blend of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) – essentially recreating the chemical “signature” of the surrounding environment.  The VOCs are generated by precisely controlled reactions within microfluidic channels, utilizing bio-engineered bacteria and enzymes to synthesize scents based on reconstructed atmospheric conditions.  

Finally, integrated haptic feedback, delivered through subtle, oscillating vibrations, mimics the physical sensations experienced by those who interacted with the object - the shifting of sediment, the impact of water, or the feel of handling the tool. The level of vibration intensity corresponds to the strength of the reconstructed geological data.
