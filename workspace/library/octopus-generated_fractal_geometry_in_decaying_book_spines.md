The “Book Spine Phenomenon” was initially documented in 2017 by Dr. Amanda Case and colleagues at the Scripps Institution of Oceanography. These patterns weren’t random; they displayed pronounced fractal characteristics – meaning smaller versions of the pattern appeared within the larger one, repeatedly.

The level of branching complexity seemed to increase with the age and decomposition of the book spines.
